Welcome

This registration page will provide instructional information to assist you and monitor your progress towards degree completion here at the University of Michigan.

Registration Steps

Step #1: Schedule Planning

  1. Visit Atlas to review course information such as average grade, professor ratings, and time slots.
  2. Access your Degree Audit to review course progress and identify remaining course requirements.
  3. Visit the UMSI Course Catalog to review semester offerings, course descriptions, and most recent syllabi.
  4. Build a sample schedule for the term using Schedule Builder.
  5. Contact your Academic Advisor to review your plans.

Step #2: Backpacking

  1. Sign into Wolverine Access.
  2. Click on "Student Business" under the "Students" tab.
  3. Click on "Student Center".
  4. Add the courses you have selected for the term to the Backpack.

Advisory Tip: Add more courses than necessary to your Backpack to ensure you have backup options if other courses fill quickly.

Step #3: Registration

  1. Sign into Wolverine Access.
  2. Click on "Student Business" under the "Students" tab.
  3. Click on "Student Center".
  4. Select the "check box" next to the courses that you have added to your backpack.
  5. Click the "Proceed to Step 2 of 3".
  6. Click "Finish Registration".
  7. Confirm that there is a green checkmark next to the course for which you intend to register. If there is a red "X", review the notification and proceed to the next steps.
  8. Lastly, view your overall schedule by selecting "My Course Schedule" within the Student Center and confirm your classes.

Monitoring Progress

You can utilize your Degree Audit Report to monitor your progress over the years by following these simple steps:

  1. Sign into Wolverine Access.
  2. Select "Student Business" under the "Students" tab.
  3. From the drop-down menu under "Academics," select "Academic Requirements".
  4. Select the double arrow button to access your Degree Audit Report.
