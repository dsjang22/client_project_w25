Definition

ARE YOU A FIRST-GENERATED COLLEGE STUDENT?

The undergraduates and graduates here at the University of Michigan are considered first-generation college students if neither parent has completed a 4-year college degree.
This includes students with other family members like siblings, cousins, and grandparents who have completed a four-year degree.

First-Generation Gateway

WHAT IS THE FIRST-GEN GATEWAY?

The First Generation Student Gateway serves as a starting point to get settled and connected to many resources for any first-generation students.

This space is used for:

  • Finding resources for first-generation students
  • Connecting to partner offices that support first-generation students
  • Meet with First Generation Project Manager Terra Molengraff
  • Meet with other students to study with one another

Hours: Monday - Friday from 9:00 AM to 3:00 PM
Location: Third floor of the Student Activities Building in the Office of Academic Multicultural Initiatives
Contact more at firstgeninfo@umich.edu
